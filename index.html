Process

Three steps to test yourself

Simple, clean, and distraction-free. No logins needed to jump in.

01
Enter your email

Drop in your email to get started. No password, no account — just your email to track your session.

02
Answer 15 questions

Race through 15 trivia questions fetched live from the Open Trivia Database. A countdown timer keeps you on your toes.

03
See your report

Get a detailed breakdown of every answer — correct, wrong, and skipped — with your final score and performance grade.

Features

Built for the experience

Every detail designed to make the quiz feel fast, fair, and fun.

Live question fetching

Questions are pulled fresh from the Open Trivia DB on every session — so no two quizzes are the same.

Countdown timer

A visible countdown adds pressure and auto-submits the quiz when time's up — no going back.

Question navigation

Jump to any question at any time. A color-coded panel shows visited, attempted, and unanswered questions.

Detailed results report

After submission, review every answer — what you got right, what you missed, and your overall score percentage.
